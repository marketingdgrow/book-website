2010 Founded

A publishing house that says yes when others look away

We started Varthamanan Pathippagam in 2010 with one idea — Tamil readers deserved better. Not just better books, but books written by people who actually knew their subject, printed with care, and priced fairly.

Over 10 years, we have grown into one of Tamil Nadu's most respected publishing houses. 480+ titles. 120+ authors. 30+ subject categories — from TNPSC and UPSC exam guides to Tamil literature, history, and essays that most publishers would never touch.

We are not just a book company. We are a platform for Tamil knowledge to reach every reader it deserves to reach.

Mission, Vision & Values

Three pillars that guide every book we publish and every author we work with.

Our Mission

To publish Tamil books that genuinely serve the reader — whether they are preparing for an exam, exploring history, or reading for the joy of it. Every book we publish must answer one question: does this truly help?

Our Vision

A Tamil Nadu where every student, every researcher, every curious reader has access to high-quality books in their language — without compromise on depth, accuracy, or relevance.

Our Values

Tamil medium first. Depth over volume. Scholar-written, reader-first. We believe a book should earn its place on the shelf — and we hold ourselves to that standard with every title we publish.

What we publish

Six pillars of Tamil knowledge — each one backed by scholar-written content and real-world relevance.

Competitive Exam Guides

TNPSC, UPSC, SSC, Banking, Railway — our exam books are written by subject experts and revised every edition. No stale content.

Tamil Literature

Classic texts, new voices, essays, short stories — we actively support Tamil literary writing that deserves to reach more readers.

History & Culture

Tamil history, Indian polity, ancient civilisations, local heritage — these are subjects we take seriously with every title.

Academic Research

We publish research works, university-level texts, and in-depth studies that would otherwise struggle to find a Tamil-medium publisher.

Current Affairs

Monthly and annual current affairs books — updated rigorously and written specifically for Tamil-medium competitive exam aspirants.

First-Time Authors

We actively work with first-time Tamil writers. If your manuscript is honest and your subject matters, we want to hear from you.
